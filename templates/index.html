About the App

This app is designed to help you learn Japanese efficiently with interactive flashcards and quizzes.

Tips for Learning a Language

  • Practice daily to build consistency.
  • Use flashcards to memorize vocabulary.
  • Watch Japanese movies or anime with subtitles.
  • Engage in conversations with native speakers.

Explore Japan: Top 4 Cities

  • Tokyo - The bustling capital, full of culture and modern attractions.
  • Kyoto - The heart of traditional Japan with stunning temples.
  • Osaka - Famous for its food scene and vibrant nightlife.
  • Hiroshima - A historical city with deep cultural significance.
