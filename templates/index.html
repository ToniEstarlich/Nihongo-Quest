Mastering the Art of Self-Coaching

練習

Practice daily to build consistency. Learning a language requires dedication. Set aside at least 30 minutes each day to study grammar, vocabulary, and sentence structure. Developing a routine will help reinforce your skills and make learning a habit.

記憶

Use flashcards to memorize vocabulary. Flashcards are a powerful tool for memorization. Apps like Anki or physical flashcards can help you retain words more effectively. Try the spaced repetition method to review words at optimal intervals for long-term retention.

視聴

Watch Japanese movies or anime with subtitles. Immersing yourself in native media improves listening skills. Start with subtitles in your native language, then switch to Japanese subtitles to train your brain. Pay attention to pronunciation and common phrases used in daily conversation.

会話

Engage in conversations with native speakers. Speaking is crucial for fluency. Use language exchange apps like Tandem or HelloTalk to practice with native speakers. Don't be afraid to make mistakes—learning happens through communication!

Vibrant culture: Top 4 Cities

Tokyo

The bustling capital of Japan, where ancient traditions blend with cutting-edge technology. From the historic Asakusa district to the futuristic skyline of Shinjuku,
Tokyo offers an unforgettable mix of culture, entertainment, and cuisine.

Kyoto

Once the imperial capital, Kyoto is the heart of traditional Japan.
Famous for its serene temples, stunning gardens, and the enchanting geisha districts of Gion, Kyoto invites visitors to step back in time and experience Japan’s rich cultural heritage.

Osaka

Known as Japan’s "Kitchen," Osaka is a paradise for food lovers, offering local specialties like takoyaki and okonomiyaki. Beyond its culinary scene, the city boasts a lively nightlife,
historic landmarks like Osaka Castle, and the thrilling entertainment district of Dotonbori.

Hiroshima

A city of resilience and peace, Hiroshima is home to the moving Peace Memorial Park and Museum.
Beyond its historical significance, visitors can explore the scenic Miyajima Island, known for its floating torii gate and breathtaking natural beauty.
